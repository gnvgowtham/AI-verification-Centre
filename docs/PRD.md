# AI Verification Center

Version: 0.1

---

# Vision

Build a trustworthy AI-powered verification platform that helps people distinguish authentic digital information from misinformation using transparent evidence, explainable AI, and trusted sources.

---

# Mission

Protect people from misinformation by making digital verification simple, transparent, and accessible.

---

# Problem Statement

People consume information from social media, messaging apps, websites, images, videos, and AI-generated content every day.

As AI-generated content becomes increasingly realistic, distinguishing authentic information from manipulated or misleading content becomes more difficult.

Current AI assistants primarily answer questions.

Our platform is specifically designed to verify information using trusted evidence.

---

# Target Users

Primary

- General Internet Users
- Students
- Journalists
- Teachers

Future

- Companies
- Government
- News Organizations

---

# Core Principles

1. Evidence over opinions.

2. Transparency over confidence.

3. If the system doesn't know, it should say so.

4. Every conclusion should be explainable.

5. Information changes over time.

6. User trust is more important than speed.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Become the most trusted AI-powered verification platform.
